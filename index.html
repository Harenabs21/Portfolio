About me

Who Am I?

Hello, my name is Harena Bijoux Safira Sarobidy and I am from Madagascar. I've studied at Haute École d'Informatique where I recently got my degree in Software Ecosystem.

Actually, I'm a Freelancer and a Frontend Web Developer. I build the user interface of a website or a web application. I have chosen especially the frontend developement because I consider it as a ART. Then, I started to be passionate about it. My dream is to become a great developer and designer like many who are in this field.
